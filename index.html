About Us

Lux Shyam Kolkata Tigers are a proud franchise in the Bengal Pro T20 League representing the Kolkata and Hooghly districts of West Bengal. The men’s team is captained by Abishek Porel while Mita Paul will wear the armband for the women’s team.

About our franchise

The Lux Shyam Kolkata Tigers franchise is co-owned by Shyam Steel Group and Lux Cozi.

Our Mission

The Kolkata franchise, one of the largest in the Bengal Pro T20 league, draws on the popularity and vibrant spirit of the city of joy. With a mission to nurture the city's abundant talent and create pathways for them to represent the country, the franchise also aims to maintain a larger-than-life presence by fostering an emotional connection with the city's residents, the franchise aspires to build a new sporting community that will elevate sports in Bengal

Our owners

Our owners

Lux Cozi

Founded in 1957 by Mr. Giridhari Lal Todi as Biswanath Hosiery Mills, Lux Cozi came into being in 1995 and a new era began when Mr Girdharilal Todi ji's sons took over the reign of the company. Lux eventually became the most preferred innerwear brand in the country for everyone. The next 20 years saw the company growing 100 times in size through a series of marketing and manufacturing innovations. This included launching the consumer coupon scheme in 1992 (Mazedar Mauka). In the same year, Lux became the first company in the industry to use celebrity brand ambassadors.

Shyam Steel Group

Established in 1953, Shyam Steel Group is a prominent producer of tmt rebars in India. The company manufactures high-quality tmt rebars, billets, sponge iron, and ferro alloys, guided by a philosophy centered on producing safe and sustainable steel products. Shyam steel group is at the forefront of quality steel manufacturing with state-of-the-art technology to fulfil the stringent requirements of its valued customers. The company has recently achieved the prestigious greenpro certification, conferred by the cii-green products and services council, for its fe 500d and fe 550d products. This certification underscores Shyam Steel Group's commitment to sustainability and environmental responsibility. The company provides a diverse

Range of products to various sectors, such as government and civil bodies, big infra projects, and individual consumers. Shyam Steel Group's dedication to quality, innovation, and sustainability has solidified its position as one of the leading tmt rebar producers in India.

Bengal T20 Pro League

Organized by Cricket Association of Bengal (CAB), the Bengal Pro T20 League is the official T20 league of West Bengal. Mentored by cricket legends Sourav Ganguly and Juhlan Goswami, this league aims to further enhance the legacy of the state as a cricketing powerhouse by producing national level cricketers.
